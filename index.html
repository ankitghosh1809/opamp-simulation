How it works

The op-amp operates in open-loop mode — no negative feedback. With gain ≥100 dB, even a µV difference between Vin and Vref saturates the output to a supply rail instantly.

Hysteresis (Schmitt Trigger) adds a dead-band via positive feedback, preventing output chatter when the signal oscillates near the threshold.
